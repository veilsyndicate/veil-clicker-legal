Privacy Policy – Veil Clicker

Effective date: 2026-01-03 · Version: 1.0
Data Controller: Arczi / the Veil Syndicate
Contact (privacy): theveilsyndicate@gmail.com

1) What data we collect

Veil Clicker may process the following data to operate the game and its Telegram integration:

  • Identifiers & linking: installId (generated by the game installation), telegramId (Telegram user ID) when you link your Telegram account.
  • Gameplay data: mission/round number, result (success/fail), grade (e.g., GOLD/SILVER/BRONZE), and pending submissions status (offline queue).
  • Technical & security logs: IP address and basic request metadata (timestamps, status codes, error logs) used for stability and abuse prevention.

We do not intentionally collect your real name, phone number, contact list, precise location, or address.

2) Why we use your data

  • To provide and maintain game functionality.
  • To link your game installation to your Telegram account and display “Linked” status.
  • To send and validate point submissions to the Veil Telegram bot.
  • To prevent abuse, duplication, and security incidents.
  • To troubleshoot crashes, bugs, and reliability issues.

3) Legal basis

Depending on your jurisdiction, we process data based on providing the service (game + integration) and our legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, diagnostics).

4) Sharing of data

We may share or disclose data only as needed to operate the service:

  • Telegram – for linking and bot communication.
  • Hosting/Infrastructure providers – to run the backend API and logs.
  • Google Play / Google services – for distribution and related technical processing.

If ads are enabled in the future (e.g., AdMob), advertising partners may process data under their own policies. This policy will be updated accordingly.

5) Data retention

  • Linking data (installId ↔ telegramId): kept as long as needed for integration or until deletion is requested.
  • Gameplay / mission reward records: kept as needed for accounting, integrity, and anti-duplication rules.
  • Technical logs: kept for a limited period for diagnostics and security.

6) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and restricted access. We avoid logging sensitive tokens or secrets.

7) Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and (where applicable) portability of your data.

To submit a privacy request, contact: theveilsyndicate@gmail.com

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes (e.g., new features, analytics, ads). The latest version will always be available at the same URL.
